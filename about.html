ABOUT THE LEGACY

Mechanical mmxxvi is the annual national level technical symposium organized by the Department of Mechanical Engineering. Established in 2007, it has grown to become one of the most anticipated events in the region.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, fostering a spirit of innovation and camaraderie among future engineers.
