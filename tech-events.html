The Paper Presentation is a platform for participants to present innovative ideas, research insights, and emerging technologies in engineering and science. MOTO PICKER

Individual

Moto Picker is an engaging technical event that tests participants’ knowledge and understanding of automotive components and systems. It challenges students to identify, analyze, and select the correct mechanical parts through logical thinking and technical awareness. The event aims to strengthen core automotive fundamentals in an interactive and competitive environment.

RAPID CAD

Individual

Rapid CAD is a design-focused technical event that challenges participants to create accurate and efficient 2D/3D models within a limited time. It tests creativity, speed, and practical understanding of computer-aided design tools. The event aims to enhance real-time design skills and problem-solving ability in engineering applications.
